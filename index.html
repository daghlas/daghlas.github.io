My Tax Administrator

Tax administration has always been a controversial topic of discussion across the various nations. As citizens we tend to express discontent with taxes due to the percieved reduction in our disposable income and the tangible impact on personal finances. However, taxes are indispensable for the functioning of a government, providing the necessary revenue to fund public services, infrastructure, and social programs. Despite our aversion to taxes, acknowledging our importance is crucial for sustaining a well-functioning society. During my time in KRA as an intern, I developed this MiniPRN application, a mobile app that helps streamline the tax collection process by reducing redundancies through incorporating of QR Codes on the payment slips and intergrated the app with the Lipa Na Mpesa API to prcoess all mpesa payments. This saves tax payers' time and effort during the process and clearings can be done much faster without any hiccups whatsoever due the streamlined process offered by this mobile application.
